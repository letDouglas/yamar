# Yamar - Your Destination for Handmade Musical Instruments

Welcome to Yamar, a unique e-commerce platform dedicated to showcasing and selling handmade musical instruments. Our mission is to connect musicians and music lovers with artisans who create beautiful, handcrafted instruments like flutes, ney, charangos, and more. This README provides an overview of our project and the innovative technologies that power it.

## Overview

At Yamar, we celebrate the art of music and craftsmanship. Our platform is built on a microservices architecture, allowing us to deliver a seamless and scalable shopping experience. Key services include:

- **Product Service**: Features a diverse range of handmade musical instruments, from traditional flutes to unique charangos.
- **Order Service**: Streamlines the purchasing process, ensuring a smooth transaction for our customers.
- **Inventory Service**: Manages the availability of our handcrafted instruments, ensuring that you always find something special.
- **Notification Service**: Engages customers with timely updates and personalized offers.
- **API Gateway**: Acts as the central hub for communication between our frontend and backend services.
- **Shop Frontend**: A visually appealing and intuitive interface built with Angular 18, designed to enhance your shopping experience.

## Tech Stack

Yamar is powered by a robust set of technologies that ensure reliability and performance:

- **Spring Boot**: The backbone of our backend services, providing a solid framework for development.
- **Angular**: For crafting a dynamic and responsive frontend that captivates users.
- **MongoDB**: A NoSQL database that allows us to store and manage our diverse product catalog efficiently.
- **MySQL**: For handling structured data related to orders and customer information.
- **Kafka**: Ensures smooth communication between services with its reliable messaging system.
- **Keycloak**: Provides secure authentication and authorization for our users.
- **Test Containers with Wiremock**: Facilitates effective testing of our services to ensure quality.
- **Grafana Stack**: Comprises Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, and Tempo for comprehensive monitoring and logging.
- **Kubernetes**: Orchestrates our containerized applications, enabling easy deployment and scaling.
